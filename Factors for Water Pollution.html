Factors For Water Pollution

Problems Faced Due to water pollution
  • There are many factors for water pollution.
  • Access to clean water sources is a major problem facing by the world today.
  • Steps have to be taken to conserve this dwindling resource.
  • Even though water is a renewable resource,its overuse and pollution makes it unfit for use.

The Factors are:

  • Dishcharge of untreated or partially treated sewage.
  • Agricultural Chemicals and industrial effluents in water bodies
  • Burning of fossil fuels
  • Mining activities
  • Chemical fertilisers and pesticides
  • Dumping plastics into water bodies
