Major multi purpose projects in India and their location and uses

    1. Damodar Valley Project
  • Its location is Panchet Dam

It's uses are:
  • Flood control,
  • Provides with irrigation facility,
  • Controlling of soil erosion,
  • Promotion of agriculture, industrial and general development of the valley.
    2. Bhakra-Nangal Project
  • Its location is Bilaspur, Himachal Pradesh in northern India.

It's uses are:

  • two dams at Bhakra and Nangal
  • Electric transmission lines and
  • Bhakra canal system for irrigation
  • Nangal Hydel Channel
